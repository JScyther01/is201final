About Me

My life and family

Family

My family is incredibly important to me. I am the 2nd youngest of five kids. I have an older sister, 2 older brothers and a young sister. I was born in California but moved to Utah when I was 4. I love to spend time with my family, especially playing board games. We spend a lot of the time in nature, and love to go hiking and camping together.

Friends

I have many great friends. We are able to do a lot of great things together, including ice skating as shown in the photo. But one of my favorite things to do with my friends is to make late night burrito runs and watch movies and shows. When we aren't doing that, we will play board games or video games. Whatever we can do that lets us be around each other.

Nature

One of the most important things in my life, is the oppurtunity to spend time in nature. I love to fish, camp and go backpacking. It is always a peaceful and rewarding experience. My goal is to hike all of the major peaks in Utah, as well as to catch a fish in every state. I have a long ways to go, but these goals have lead to some of the most fun experiences of my life.
